The DataForSolution method

Learn by understanding, building, and applying

Each lesson is designed to move beyond memorization and produce practical understanding, evidence of learning, and useful work.

1

Understand

Learn the meaning of a concept and why it matters before using tools or writing code.

2

Recognize

Identify the concept in datasets, systems, decisions, and real-world situations.

3

Build

Create a worksheet, dataset, analysis, visualization, notebook, model, or practical artifact.

4

Apply

Connect the learning to a real problem, professional workflow, research question, or decision.
